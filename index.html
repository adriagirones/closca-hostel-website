About Closca

Part guesthouse, part family

Closca sits in the quiet south of Koh Tao, a short walk from Chalok Baan Kao Bay, where the water is calm and the pace is slower than the rest of the island. It's run day-to-day by a couple who greet you like a friend arriving rather than a booking checking in — and that feeling tends to stick with people long after they leave.

Rooms are simple, spotless and quietly comfortable, the WiFi is fast enough to work from the terrace, and the crowd is a mix of divers, remote workers and backpackers who all end up sharing the same coffee table by evening.

  • Steps from Chalok Baan Kao Bay
  • Clean, comfortable rooms
  • Genuinely friendly, hands-on hosts
  • Fast WiFi, built for remote work
  • Free daily breakfast included
  • Loved by divers & nomads alike

Explore

Koh Tao is your backyard

Everything below is reachable on foot, by bike, or a short longtail ride away.

World-class diving

Calm, current-free water right off Chalok Bay makes it one of the easiest places on Earth to learn to dive — or rack up advanced certifications.

Beaches & snorkeling

Freedom Beach, Buddha Rock and Sai Nuan are all a short stroll away, each with its own patch of reef worth a mask and fins.

Sunsets & viewpoints

John Suwan Viewpoint is a ten-minute walk from your room — one of the best panoramas in the Gulf of Thailand, especially at golden hour.

Restaurants & nightlife

Beach bars, family-run Thai kitchens and the occasional bonfire party — Chalok keeps it relaxed while Sairee's nightlife is a quick ride north.
