Stay

Koh Tao hostel rooms & dorms for every kind of traveller

From private rooms with a bay breeze to sociable dorms built for meeting people — every bed at Closca Hostel comes with air conditioning, fast WiFi and a good night's sleep, steps from Chalok Baan Kao Bay, Koh Tao.

Standard Private Room

2 Guests

A tidy private room for travellers who want their own space without the resort price tag — a comfortable double bed, air conditioning and a desk if you need to get work done before the beach. Three of our four Standard rooms are set up this way. Serviced every two days, with free towels always on hand.

  • Air conditioning
  • Private bathroom
  • Double bed
  • Free WiFi
  • Work desk
  • Free towels

Standard Twin Room

2 Guests

The same tidy comfort as our Standard Private Room, set up with two separate single beds instead of one double — a good fit for friends, colleagues or family travelling together. It's the one of our four Standard rooms built this way, so it's worth requesting early.

  • Air conditioning
  • Private bathroom
  • Twin beds
  • Free WiFi
  • Work desk
  • Free towels

Deluxe Private Room

2 Guests

More room to breathe, a little more light, and — in most cases — a balcony to catch the mountain air. The upgrade pick for couples or anyone staying a while — we only have two, so they book up quickly. Serviced every two days, with free towels always on hand.

  • Air conditioning
  • Private bathroom
  • Balcony
  • Mountain view
  • Free towels
  • Serviced every 2 days

4-Bed Dorm

4 Guests

A small, sociable dorm with sturdy bunks, each with its own reading light, power outlet and locker, plus its own private bathroom with toilet and shower. Available as a mixed dorm or a female-only dorm — two of our 4-bed rooms are mixed and one is female-only, and each can also be booked out separately for a private group.

  • Air conditioning
  • Private bathroom
  • Personal locker
  • Reading light & outlet
  • Free towels
  • Mixed or female-only
